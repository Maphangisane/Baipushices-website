About Company

Baipushi Armed Security Solution was established in 2019 by JM. Mokgaphami .
Baipushi Armed Security Solution has during its one-year history developed into
South Africa’s leading, predominantly black male - owned and managed logistics
company. Baipushi is a leading provider of Social Housing in the country. One of.
Baipushi’s key focus areas is the development of young talent and today boasts
six black female shareholders who hold in excess of 54% of the company’s shares
- TRULY EMPOWERED. The company is currently registered with the PRASA as
level 9 GBPE and 9 CEPE. . Baipushi has been registered with the CIRA since its
inception and remains in good standing. Through JM. Mokgaphami’s
involvement, . Baipushi is a founder member and leading role player in SAWIAR
(South African Women In Logistics Respond). JM.Mokgaphami is the immediate
past president of the Black Business Council for the Built Environment.
