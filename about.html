About Us

Baipushi Civil, Electrical & Security is an established Private Enterprise based in the Limpopo, Gauteng, Mpumalanga Province.


It has during its existence developed into South Africa’s leading, predominantly black male-owned and managed company and a leading provider of Social Housing in the country.
One of Baipushi’s key focus areas is the development of young talent and today boasts six black female shareholders who hold in excess of 54% of the company’s shares - TRULY EMPOWERED.
The company is currently registered with the PRASA as level 9 GBPE and 9 CEPE. Baipushi has been registered with the CIRA since its inception and remains in good standing.
Baipushi is a founder member and leading role player in SAWIAR (South African Women In Armed Response).

WHAT WE DO

The company was established to offer excellent services and to play an important role in building a vibrant and sustainable economy of the Province.

VISION

To be a leading and most preferred Enterprise in the Limpopo Province.

MISSION

By providing top quality and exceptional services to our clients we shall be able to grow and take lead in the supply and construction industry.
We aim to empower the youth by imparting various skills and aligning the Enterprise with the RDP goals by providing opportunities for women, youth and the disabled.

OBJECTIVES

  • To exceed customer expectations for service performance and value.
  • To contribute towards addressing the needs of disadvantaged communities, especially women and the disabled.
  • To create new employment opportunities for the unemployed people in the country.
  • To attain a strong cash position and achieve profitability on an annual basis
  • To build long term relationships with our clients

CORE VALUES

  • Demonstrate a high level of professionalism
  • To provide excellent services at all times
  • To conduct our business with honesty and integrity
  • To treat customers information with confidentiality
  • To be always innovative

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION

We believe that customer satisfaction is vital to our success. Our customer satisfaction model is based on the following principles:

  • Convenience
  • Friendly and Professional Service
  • Safety and Security
  • Commitment
  • Dependability and Reliability

NETWORKING AND LINKAGES

The success of Baipushi Trading Enterprise will depend, inter alia, on the level of networking and linkages with other businesses and support organizations.
We aim to form close relationships with public institutions such as LIMDEV, LIBSA, SEDA, NYDA, Municipalities and Government Departments in-so-far as SMME development is concerned.
